CodeZri

Open-source software, tutorials, and articles for developers!

CodeZri is making open-source products and great content for developers around the world. We built amazing free and open-source products including Neutralinojs and Jerverless. We often publish coding tutorials and articles about facts for your curiosity.

We are not indeed yet another group that publishes articles and videos about the latest technologies. Our goal is to produce great content that will make everyone outstanding developers. We also love to innovate solutions for problems that developers face every day. For example, we built Neutralinojs for the hybrid bloatware problem.

Our favourite study areas are desktop application development, cloud computing, JavaScript, C/C++, Linux, data structures and algorithms.
